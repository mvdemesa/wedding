How It All Began
Together, Always

Our love story began with a class on Benedictine Spirituality during our MBA at San Beda University. Of all places, a lecture hall was where a connection first sparked — quiet proof that the best things often arrive when we least expect them.

Our first real conversation happened over coffee at the Starbucks on Visayas Avenue. One cup turned into many, and what began as classmates grew into a partnership we now get to celebrate. As we stand on the threshold of our wedding day, we can't wait to begin the next chapter, writing our happily ever after together.
